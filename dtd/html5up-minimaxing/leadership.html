Athletics & Leadership

Delts at Tufts strive to be leaders and influencers in any environment, whether athletic or extracurricular. Our chapter is well represented in varsity and club athletics at Tufts. We currently have athletes participating on the varsity rowing, track and field, squash and golf teams. Additionally, we have athletes and leaders in club lacrosse and volleyball teams. Many brothers not involvied in athletics at a varsity or club level choose to form intramural sports teams, often competing in soccer, basketball and flag football.

Our brotherhhod also has a huge engagement with many non-athletic groups on campus. These vary from musical and performance groups, pre-professional and consulting groups, and social impact clubs.
